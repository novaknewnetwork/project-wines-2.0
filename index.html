Exclusive to Hedonism - Capreolus Apricot Eau de Vie 2021

Based in the heart of the British countryside, Capreolus are a boutique distillery who focus on producing small batch, delicately perfumed, complex Eau de Vie and Gin. Founded in 2014 by Barney Wilczak, the distillery has swiftly gained a reputation for producing high quality distillates that rival the finest fruit brandies in the world.

We were delighted to collaborate with Barney on an exclusive bottling of their 2021 apricot Eau de Vie, limited to just 120 bottles.

The Dalmore

For over 150 years, The Dalmore has set the standard in multi cask maturation. Hand selected casks, from the world's finest bodegas and wineries are expertly curated to create a sumptuous and layered whisky of legendary finesse.

From their core range to their incredible single vintage Constellation bottlings, each expression presents a different nuance of the distillery's definitive house style.

The Fine Wines of California

Ever since Steven Spurrier's famous Judgement of Paris in the 1970s, the wine world has held the fine wines of California in the same regard as the very best examples from Bordeaux and Burgundy. Names like Screaming Eagle, Marcassin, Shafer Vineyards, Abreu and Harlan take pride of place on the wine lists of some of the greatest restaurants and in the cellars of connoisseurs worldwide.

Here at Hedonism we have over 1,000 different wines from California including rare vintages back to the 1970s and cult wines unavailable anywhere else.

American Bourbon, Whiskey and Rye

From Buffalo Trace's annual Antique Collection to Blanton's highly sought after single barrel bottlings, the American whiskey renaissance is in full swing! We have over 250 to choose from at Hedonism Wines including vintage bottlings back to the 1950s and long-discontinued limited editions.

Dive in and pick out a bottle to enjoy - whether your preference is for a rich, smooth Kentucky Bourbon or a spicy, complex rye we have something for everyone.

Fine Wine and Spirits Boutique
